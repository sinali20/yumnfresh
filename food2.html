Recipe Catelog

Discover a world of culinary delights by perusing our wide recipe library. We provide a broad range of delectable and healthful alternatives to accommodate all tastes and dietary requirements, ranging from appetizers to main courses and desserts to beverages.

Appetizers

Spinach and Feta Stuffed Mushrooms

Try our Spinach and Feta Stuffed Mushrooms and experience the harmonious combination of earthy mushrooms, bright spinach, and the rich creaminess of feta cheese. This appetizer is a delicious bite-sized experience, with a balanced combination of flavors and textures.

Caprese Skewers

These Caprese Skewers retain the essential elements of a typical Caprese salad, but are turned into a simple and delicious appetiser or snack. They're tasty and gorgeous, and they're ideal for Christmas, Sunday breakfast, a summer get-together, or any other event!

Mains

Grilled Salmon with Lemon Butter Sauce

Grilled Salmon with Garlic Lemon Butter Sauce is one of the most delicious and simple dishes you'll ever make! Tender salmon fillets are pan-fried till golden brown before being topped with a simple, lively, and delicious lemon butter sauce.

Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

This Quinoa Stuffed Peppers dish uses plant-based protein such as quinoa and beans to create a nutritious, hearty, and tasty lunch that both vegetarians and non-vegetarians will enjoy! Peppers can be stuffed with almost anything, and beef stuffed peppers are a family favorite!

Desserts

Berry Chia Seed Pudding

Better stock up on those chia seeds cause you’re going to love this Berry Chia Pudding. Made with a triple berry punch, this healthy snack or breakfast recipe is full of protein and fiber to keep you full for hours.



Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Avocado chocolate mousse, made with just a handful of ingredients and only 5 minutes of prep time. Rich, creamy, and oh-so-chocolatey, this luscious vegan mousse is perfect for entertaining or a decadent dessert. It’s gluten-free and egg-free too!
